Hi, I’m Kyle. I’m a music producer.

I use the following software:

  • Ableton Live
  • Avid Pro Tools
  • Adobe Audition

What excites me most about production is collaboration with others. There is no better feeling than sitting down with a musician and sensing chemistry between their ideas and mine.

My ultimate goal every time I start a project is to translate musical ideas into a fully-realized track that is both original and professional.

In high school I began collecting hardware samplers and records and attending shows in the greater Boston area. What started as a casual interest quickly became a full-time pursuit.

Since then, I have produced dozens of tracks, exchanged project files with collaborators across the world, and DJed a handful of events and parties.

I am currently exploring interactive web technology as a means of creating a more immersive audiovisual experience.
